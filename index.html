The Problem

International Trade Is Still
Complicated

Finding genuine partners, managing documentation, arranging logistics — the current system is fragmented, risky, and time-consuming for businesses of all sizes.

🔍

Finding Genuine Buyers & Suppliers

Exporters spend months searching for verified international buyers without any way to confirm legitimacy or trade capability.

⚠️

Fraud & Verification Risk

Fake companies, fraudulent documentation, and unverified counterparties cost businesses millions every year in global trade.

📄

Documentation Complexity

IEC, RCMC, APEDA, shipping bills, LoC, phytosanitary certificates — navigating compliance is a full-time job in itself.

🚛

Logistics Coordination

Coordinating freight forwarders, customs agents, last-mile delivery, and warehousing across borders is expensive and complex.

💸

Payment Uncertainty

International payment terms, currency risk, and absence of escrow arrangements leave businesses exposed to default risk.

🗂️

Compliance Verification

Verifying that a counterparty holds the right registrations and is compliant with export/import regulations is manual and opaque.
